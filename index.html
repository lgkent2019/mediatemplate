TYPES OF MEDIA

Animation

One type of media is animation. We can convey many thoughts using animation that we would not be able to any other way. Animation uses the imagination like nothing else.

Cinema

Another form of media is film. There are both fiction and nonfiction forms of cinema. One can tell their version of the truth using film without fear of being interupteed. One can also tell a story they created for the enjoyment of all who care to take it in.

Thunderstorm

Thunderstorms! The sound of rain hitting our surroundings. The clap of thunder followed by a flash of lightning. Some people fear them. I find them relaxing. So much so, that I have an app that will play the thunderstorm while I doze off into dream land. Many apps and videos provide this soothing sound. One such app is White Noise - Lite.
